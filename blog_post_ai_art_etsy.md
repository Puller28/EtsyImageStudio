## Mastering AI Prompts for Commercial Success

### The AIDA Prompt Framework

**A**esthetics: Define the visual style
**I**mage: Describe the main subject
**D**etails: Add specific elements
**A**spect: Specify technical requirements

**Example:**
"Minimalist botanical illustration [Aesthetics], single eucalyptus branch [Image], watercolor style with soft green tones [Details], clean white background, high resolution [Aspect]"

### Proven Prompt Formulas

**Style + Subject + Mood + Technical**
- "Modern abstract art, geometric shapes, calming and peaceful, 4K resolution"

**Art Movement + Subject + Color Palette + Format**
- "Art Deco style, vintage car, gold and black color scheme, poster format"

**Medium + Subject + Composition + Background**
- "Watercolor painting, mountain landscape, rule of thirds, gradient sky background"

### Advanced Prompting Techniques

**Negative Prompts**: Exclude unwanted elements
- Example: "beautiful flower --no people, text, watermarks"

**Style References**: Mention specific artists or movements
- Example: "in the style of Monet, impressionist technique"

**Technical Specifications**: Ensure quality output
- Example: "8K resolution, professional photography, studio lighting"

**Emotional Descriptors**: Add mood and feeling
- Example: "serene, peaceful, meditative, zen-like atmosphere"

## Legal and Ethical Considerations

### Copyright and AI Art

**What You Own:**
- AI-generated images you create with prompts
- Derivative works based on AI outputs
- Commercial rights to sell and license

**What You Don't Own:**
- Copyright to the AI training data
- Exclusive rights to the AI model's outputs
- Protection against similar AI-generated works

### Best Practices for Commercial Use

**Always Check Platform Terms:**
- Review each AI platform's commercial use policies
- Understand attribution requirements
- Keep records of your subscription status

**Avoid Copyrighted References:**
- Don't use specific character names or brands
- Avoid prompting for celebrity likenesses
- Create original concepts rather than copying existing art

**Disclosure Considerations:**
- Some platforms require AI disclosure
- Etsy doesn't mandate AI disclosure for digital art
- Consider transparency as a marketing advantage

**Resolution Standards:**
- Minimum: 300 DPI for print quality
- Recommended: 4000x3000 pixels or higher
- Format: High-quality JPEG or PNG
