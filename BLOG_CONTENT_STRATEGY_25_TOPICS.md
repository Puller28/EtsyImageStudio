## üìù **Content Guidelines**

### **Word Count**
- Minimum: 1,500 words
- Optimal: 2,000-2,500 words
- Maximum: 3,500 words

### **Structure**
- H1: Main title (keyword-optimized)
- H2: Major sections (4-6)
- H3: Subsections
- Bullet points for scannability
- Images every 300-400 words
- Table of contents for long posts

### **SEO Elements**
- Meta title (60 characters max)
- Meta description (155 characters max)
- Focus keyword in first 100 words
- Internal links (3-5 per post)
- External links (2-3 authoritative sources)
- Alt text for all images
- Schema markup (FAQ, How-To)

### **Engagement Elements**
- Actionable tips and checklists
- Screenshots and examples
- Before/after comparisons
- Case studies and testimonials
- Downloadable resources
- Video tutorials (where applicable)
- CTA to try Art Studio
