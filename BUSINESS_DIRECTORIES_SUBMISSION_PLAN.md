## üí° **Pro Tips**

### **1. Optimize Listings for Conversion**
- Use action-oriented language
- Highlight Etsy-specific benefits
- Include pricing transparency
- Add "Start Free Trial" CTAs

### **2. Collect Reviews Proactively**
- Email customers after successful projects
- Offer incentives for honest reviews
- Respond to all reviews (positive & negative)
- Aim for 4.5+ star average

### **3. Keep Listings Updated**
- Add new features monthly
- Update screenshots quarterly
- Refresh descriptions with new keywords
- Announce major updates

### **4. Cross-Promote**
- Link directories to each other
- Share directory profiles on social media
- Include in email signatures
- Mention in blog posts
